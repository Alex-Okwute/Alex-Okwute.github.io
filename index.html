Coconut Rice

  • Time: 45mins
  • Serves: 4 Persons
Ingredients Quantity
Coconuts 2 pcs
Rice 3 cups
Pepper 5 balls
Onion 2 bulbs
Maggi 2 cubes
Salt 0.25 g

Step 1: Break Coconut and remove shell.
Step 2: Cut coconut into sizeable pieces, pour into blender, add water and blend till smooth.
Step 3: Sieve to get juice and throw away the chaff.
Step 4: Pour coconut juice into pot, add sliced onion and pepper. Bring to boil.
Step 5: Add rice, maggi and salt to taste.
Step 6: Reduce heat and cook till rice is soft.

Akara (Beans Balls)

  • Time: 30mins
  • Serves: 3 Persons
Ingredients Quantity
Beans 500g
Oil 0.3 ltrs
Fresh Pepper 3 balls
Onion 1 bulb
Warm Water 0.45 ltrs
Salt 0.2 g

Step 1: Soak beans for 5 mins and wash thoroughly to remove skin.
Step 2: Grind beans until smooth.
Step 3: Place beans paste in a large bowl. Season with salt and beat in warm water a little at a time to make it light.
Step 4: Fold in sliced onions and mix with a metal spoon.
Step 5: Heat oil until blue faint smoke appears. Spoon mixture into the oil to form balls until golden brown.
Step 6: Drain on absorbent kitchen paper. Serve hot with chilli sauce.

Spicy Scrambled Egg

  • Time: 20mins
  • Serves: 4 Persons
Ingredients Quantity
Eggs 8 pcs
Ground-Oil 3 tbs
Chilli Pepper 1 pc
Onion 1 bulb
Green Pepper 3 pcs
Crushed Garlic 1 clove
Thyme 1 tbs
Tomatoes 4 pcs
Salt 0.2 g

Step 1: Heat oil in a frying pan. Addonion and cook until it begins to colour.
Step 2: Add pepper and cook for 5 mins, then add chopped tomatoes, garlic, chilli and other seasoning.
Step 3: Stir and cook for another 5 mins`.
Step 4: Meanwhile, lightly whisk the eggs with a little salt and stir into the tomatoes.
Step 5: Reduce heat and cook, stirring constantly until creamy and wekk combined for about 8 mins.
Step 6: Sprinkle with chopped cori-ander and serve hot.

Plantain Chips

  • Time: 15mins
  • Serves: 2 Persons
Ingredients Quantity
Plantain (Half Ripened) 4 pcs
Oil 0.3 ltrs
Salt 0.25 tbs

Step 1: Peel plantain and cut in half.
Step 2: Slice thinly and diagonally or into rounds. Sprinkle with salt.
Step 3: Heat oil and and deep fry the slices of plantain until crisp and golden.
Step 4: Drain and serve as an accompaniment to some drink or a snack.
